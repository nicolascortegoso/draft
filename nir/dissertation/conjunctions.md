Two-types of connections: coordinated and subordinate.

COORDINATING 
Regarding their meaning, coordinating conjunctions have a relation with particles. Words like да, даҕаны, эрэ, дуу, combine the meaning of particles and conjunctions. They are consedered omonims.

In general, conjunctions differ from particles in that their meaning is associated not with a single word or element in the sentence, but to both speech unities.

They also have much in common with modal words regarding their meaining and use.

There is a group of words and word combinations that have a transitional status between conjunctions and modal words. Most of this words principally act as modals, showing a conjunctional meaning in different degrees.
